The Studio

Enigma Creation Design was born from a simple belief: that the jewelry you wear every day should mean something. Founded in 2018, our small studio has crafted thousands of pieces for customers across 40+ countries.

We work with sterling silver, gold-plated brass, rose gold, and genuine gemstones. Every bracelet is knotted, every ring is shaped, every pendant is soldered by hand. No machines. No mass production.

When you wear an Enigma piece, you carry a small mystery — a story only you know. That's exactly how we want it.
