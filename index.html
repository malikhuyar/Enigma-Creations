Our Story

Born from Mystery,
Crafted with Soul

It began with a question: what if jewelry could hold secrets?

Enigma Creation Design was born in 2018 from a studio in Portland, Oregon — a quiet space where ancient symbolism meets modern artistry. Founder Maya Chen had spent years studying gemology and sacred geometry, fascinated by how civilizations across time used adornment to mark the unexplainable.

Maya's jewelry doesn't just decorate — it speaks. Each collection is inspired by a theme: celestial bodies, ancient runes, the geometry of nature. The pieces are small philosophies, worn close to the body, whispering meaning only the wearer can decipher.

Today, Enigma Creation Design ships to over 40 countries. Every piece is still handcrafted in our studio, using ethically sourced metals and stones. We believe jewelry should be a conversation — between maker and material, between wearer and world.

🤲

Handcrafted Integrity

No mass production. Every piece is shaped, soldered, and polished by hand in our Portland studio.

🌍

Ethical Sourcing

We partner only with suppliers who share our commitment to fair labor and environmental responsibility.

🎨

Intentional Design

Each collection is researched and designed with deep respect for its cultural and symbolic roots.
